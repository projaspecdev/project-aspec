         GT2 CarInfo Editor CSV v0.1
=================================================================
	 by pez2k
=================================================================

A tool to convert the carinfo files in Gran Turismo 2 to and from
CSV. These files contain replay names, car colour information,
car colour names and region-specific car information.

=================================================================

How to use:

Place the following GT2 files in the same directory as the exe:
.carcolor
.carinfoj
.carinfoa
.carinfoe
.ccjapanese
.cclatain

Drag any one of the above files onto the exe to convert to CSV.

Drag any one of the output CSV files onto the exe to convert them
all back into the above six files.

=================================================================

Requirements:
